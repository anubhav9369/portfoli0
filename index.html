Know About Me

Data Science grad, and a little bit of everything.

I'm Anubhav Verma, an M.Sc. Data Science student at VIT Chennai (CGPA 8.56) from Lucknow. My work spans healthcare diagnostics, misinformation detection, personal finance, and quantum-classical ML — connecting deep technical execution with real-world impact.

Currently I'm an ML Engineer intern at CDAC India and building geospatial pipelines at ShieldNex under India's IN-SPACe NGP 2024 programme. A DST INSPIRE Scholar — top 1% nationwide.

When I'm not building, I'm travelling, reading widely, writing sometimes, and riding my father's restored bike. Life's about balance — and I'm chasing range, not just depth.
